# Email Automation Checklist

## Use this to identify what to automate

---

## Section 1: Your Email Volume

1. How many emails do you receive per day?
   - [ ] Less than 20
   - [ ] 20-50
   - [ ] 50-100
   - [ ] 100+

2. How many emails do you send per day?
   - [ ] Less than 10
   - [ ] 10-30
   - [ ] 30-50
   - [ ] 50+

---

## Section 2: Email Types You Receive

3. What % of emails are from leads/prospects?
   - [ ] 0-10%
   - [ ] 10-30%
   - [ ] 30-50%
   - [ ] 50%+

4. What % are from existing clients?
   - [ ] 0-10%
   - [ ] 10-30%
   - [ ] 30-50%
   - [ ] 50%+

5. What % are newsletters/junk?
   - [ ] 0-20%
   - [ ] 20-40%
   - [ ] 40-60%
   - [ ] 60%+

---

## Section 3: Repetitive Tasks

6. Do you send the same response to similar emails?
   - [ ] Yes, daily
   - [ ] Yes, weekly
   - [ ] Sometimes
   - [ ] No

7. Do you manually forward emails to team members?
   - [ ] Yes, many times per day
   - [ ] Yes, daily
   - [ ] Occasionally
   - [ ] No

8. Do you manually add email contacts to CRM?
   - [ ] Yes, always
   - [ ] Yes, sometimes
   - [ ] Rarely
   - [ ] No (automated)

9. Do you manually schedule meetings from emails?
   - [ ] Yes, always
   - [ ] Yes, sometimes
   - [ ] Rarely
   - [ ] No (using calendar links)

---

## Section 4: Follow-up Pain

10. Do leads fall through the cracks?
    - [ ] Always
    - [ ] Often
    - [ ] Sometimes
    - [ ] Never

11. How many follow-up emails do you send per day?
    - [ ] 0-5
    - [ ] 5-10
    - [ ] 10-20
    - [ ] 20+

12. Do you have a systematic follow-up sequence?
    - [ ] No
    - [ ] Manual
    - [ ] Partially automated
    - [ ] Fully automated

---

## Section 5: Reporting

13. Do you manually compile email stats?
    - [ ] Yes, weekly
    - [ ] Yes, monthly
    - [ ] Rarely
    - [ ] No (automated)

14. Do you track email response times?
    - [ ] No
    - [ ] Sometimes
    - [ ] Yes, manually
    - [ ] Yes, automatically

---

## Score Your Automation Needs

Count your checks:

**Mostly [ ]** → High automation need
**Mostly [ ]** → Medium automation need  
**Mostly [ ]** → Low automation need

---

## Recommended Automations

### High Need:
- Auto-response to leads
- Lead categorization
- Follow-up sequences
- CRM sync

### Medium Need:
- Email routing
- Meeting scheduling
- Weekly digest

### Low Need:
- Newsletter filtering
- Auto-archive
