## Script for Discovery Call

**Opening (2 min):**
"Thanks for taking the time. Before we dive in, can you tell me briefly what prompted you to look into automation?"

**Qualification (3 min):**
- "What does a typical week look like for you?"
- "What tasks take up the most time?"
- "What's the cost of not getting these done?"

**Solution (5 min):**
"Based on what you've shared, here are a few things we could automate:
1. [Specific task]
2. [Specific task]
3. [Specific task]"

**Pricing (2 min):**
"For [X] hours of time saved per week, most clients invest [Y]. We offer one-off builds ($500-3K) or retainers ($2K-10K/mo)."

**Close:**
"Does this make sense? Want to move forward with a scope?"
