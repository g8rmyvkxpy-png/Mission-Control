# Competitor Analysis - AI Automation Services (India)

## Direct Competitors

### 1. Zapier
- **Pricing:** $19.99-599.99/month (₹1,600-50,000)
- **Strengths:** Market leader, 5K+ integrations, no-code
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive for Indian SMBs, limited AI
- **Our Edge:** Custom AI workflows, Indian pricing, personal support

### 2. Make (Integromat)
- **Pricing:** $9-299/month (₹750-25,000)
- **Strengths:** Visual workflows, good pricing
- **Weaknesses:** Learning curve, basic AI
- **Our Edge:** Done-for-you setup, AI-first approach

### 3. n8n (Self-hosted)
- **Pricing:** Free to $100/month (self-hosted)
- **Strengths:** Powerful, free option
- **Weaknesses:** Technical setup required
- **Our Edge:** We set it up for you + support
