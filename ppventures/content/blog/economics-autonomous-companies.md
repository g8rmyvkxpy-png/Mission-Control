# The Economics of Autonomous Companies

Traditional companies scale costs with revenue. Autonomous companies break that equation.

## The Traditional Cost Structure

```
Revenue → Salaries → Operations → Profit
     ↓          ↓           ↓
   30-70%     20-40%      10-20%
```

As you grow, people costs grow. Every new customer needs more support, sales, and delivery.

## The Autonomous Model

```
Revenue → AI Operations → Profit
     ↓          ↓
   30%        5-10%
```

## Real Numbers

| Metric | Traditional | Autonomous |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Customer Acquisition | $200-500 | $20-50 |
| Support Cost/Customer | $50/mo | $5/mo |
| Content Production | $5000/mo | $500/mo |
| Scalability | Linear | Exponential |

## The Compound Effect

Every AI agent you add:
- Works 24/7
- Never burns out
- Gets better over time
- Multiplies other agents' productivity

## Why This Matters Now

AI capability + cloud infrastructure + automation tools = Autonomous companies are now economically viable for anyone.

The barrier isn't technology. It's mindset.
