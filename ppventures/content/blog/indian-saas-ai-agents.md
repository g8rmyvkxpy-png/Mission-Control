- **Early-stage startups** (pre-seed to seed): Deploying 1-2 agents for lead gen and customer support

Founders see competitors launching AI features weekly. The FOMO is real.

## The Risk Nobody Talks About

Here's what keeps me up at night — and what you should watch for:

### Hallucinations
AI agents can make things up. Always add human review for customer-facing outputs.

### Data Privacy
Don't feed customer data to AI without understanding where it goes. Use on-premise options if handling sensitive data.

### Over-Automation
The goal isn't to replace humans. It's to free them for high-value work. Keep the human in the loop.
