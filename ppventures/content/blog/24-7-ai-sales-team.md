I used to spend every Monday morning doing something I hated: hunting for leads.

Two hours of scrolling through LinkedIn. Another hour checking news sites. Then drafting the same template email to 20 people who never replied.

Then I built three AI agents. Now I wake up to qualified leads in my inbox every morning.

Here's exactly how it works.

## The Three Agents

### Neo — The Lead Finder

Neo searches the web every single day for businesses that match my ideal client profile. It looks for:

- Companies that recently raised funding
- Businesses mentioned in industry news
- Startups with job openings (sign of growth)
- Founders who match my target criteria

**What I get:** 10 qualified leads every morning, with source links and qualification notes.

### Atlas — The Researcher

Before I even open an email, Atlas has already checked:

- Competitor activity
- Recent news about the lead
- Company updates that make outreach relevant

**What I get:** Context. So I'm not reaching out blind.

### Orbit — The Operations Manager

Orbit handles the grunt work:

- Sending follow-ups that didn't get replies
- Logging everything to my CRM
- Generating end-of-day reports

**What I get:** A summary at 6 PM telling me what got done.

## The Math

| Task | Before | After |
|------|--------|-------|
| Lead research | 2 hrs/day | 0 mins |
| Outreach drafting | 1 hr/day | 0 mins |
| Follow-ups | Missed constantly | Automatic |
| Weekly reporting | Sunday night panic | Done by Friday |

**Time saved:** ~15 hours/week
**Cost:** $297/month (less than an intern)

## The Setup

You don't need to be a developer. Here's what I configured:

1. **Ideal client criteria** — 2 minute form
2. **Outreach tone** — "Professional but human"
3. **Working hours** — "Run while I sleep"

That's it. The agents figured out the rest.

## What Actually Works

After 3 months, here's what I've learned:

### Quality over quantity
Neo finds 10 leads/day. Not 100. That keeps the outreach human-sized.

### Context beats templates
Atlas gives me one sentence about why now is the right time to reach out. Game changer.

### Follow-up is everything
Orbit sends 3 follow-ups over 2 weeks. Most replies come on follow-up #2 or #3.

## The Results

- **Leads generated:** 200+ this month
- **Response rate:** 18% (vs. 3% cold outreach)
- **Meetings booked:** 12
- **Revenue:** Enough to justify the $297/mo 10x over
