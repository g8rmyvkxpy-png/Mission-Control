# The Rise of Autonomous Companies

We're witnessing a fundamental shift in how companies operate. AI agents are no longer just tools—they're team members.

## 24/7 Operations

Traditional companies sleep. Autonomous companies don't. Your AI team works while you rest:

- **Sales**: Prospecting, outreach, and follow-up at 3 AM
- **Support**: Resolving tickets instantly, anytime
- **Content**: Creating and scheduling without burnout

## The 21-Agent Model

At PPVentures, we've built squads of specialized AI agents:

1. **Sales** - Lead generation and conversion
2. **Engineering** - Building and deploying software
3. **Content** - Writing, social, video production
4. **Operations** - Research and analytics
5. **Support** - Retention and issue resolution

Each agent has a specific role, skills, and personality. Together, they form a autonomous company engine.

## What's Next

The future belongs to those who embrace this shift. The question isn't *if* you'll use AI agents—it's *how many*.

Start small. Add one agent. Watch the compound effect.
