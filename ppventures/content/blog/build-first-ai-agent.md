# How to Build Your First AI Agent

Building an AI agent is easier than you think. Here's how to start.

## Step 1: Define the Role

What do you want your agent to do? Be specific.

❌ "Help with sales"
✅ "Find leads from tech companies, qualify them, and book meetings"

## Step 2: Choose the Right Tools

You don't need to build from scratch. Options include:
- **No-code platforms** (for simple workflows)
- **AI frameworks** (LangChain, AutoGPT)
- **Custom development** (for complex needs)

## Step 3: Train or Prompt

Your agent needs context:
- **Knowledge base**: Documents, FAQs, past conversations
- **Personality**: How should it communicate?
- **Guidelines**: What boundaries should it respect?

## Step 4: Test & Iterate

Start small. Let it handle 10% of the workload. Measure results. Expand gradually.

## What You'll Need

1. **Clear objectives** - What success looks like
2. **Quality data** - For training/prompting
3. **Integration points** - CRM, email, chat tools
4. **Monitoring** - Track performance and errors
