# Real Estate Agents: Stop Chasing Leads. Let AI Do It.

Amit Khanna sells premium residential properties in South Mumbai. He's good at what he does — 15 years of experience, a strong network, great negotiation skills.

But he was losing deals to something he couldn't control: time.

---

## The 5-Minute Window Every Real Estate Agent Misses

Here's what happens in real estate:

1. Someone visits your website
2. They fill out a form or WhatsApp you
3. They expect a response within minutes
4. If you don't respond in 5 minutes, their interest drops by 80%
5. By the time you call back 2 hours later, they've already seen 3 other properties

Amit was losing 35% of his leads to slow response times. Not because he was lazy — because he was with another client, in a meeting, or sleeping.

"It's embarrassing," he says. "I'd call someone back the next morning and they'd say 'I already bought something else.'"

---

## The AI Solution: 24/7 Lead Nurturing

We set up three AI agents for his agency:

1. **Instant Response Agent**
   - Replies within 30 seconds to any WhatsApp/message
   - Qualifies the lead (budget, location, timeline)
   - Schedules a call with Amit only for hot leads

2. **Follow-up Agent**
   - Nurtures cold leads automatically
   - Sends property matches based on their preferences
   - Re-engages leads who went silent

3. **Market Update Agent**
   - Sends weekly property updates to his database
   - Keeps his agency top-of-mind
   - Triggers responses from "just browsing" leads

## The Secret: Never Sound Like a Robot

Here's what makes this work in real estate: the AI doesn't sound like a bot.

It uses Amit's actual language patterns. It knows his available properties by heart. It answers questions about floor plans, possession timelines, and loan eligibility just like he would.

"We tested it extensively," Amit says. "We had customers who didn't realize they were talking to AI until we told them. They thought it was me texting from my phone."
