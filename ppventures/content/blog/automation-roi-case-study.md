Running a business in India means dealing with rising operational costs, talent shortages, and the constant pressure to do more with less. Here's how one of our clients achieved dramatic cost savings with AI automation.

## The Problem: Burning ₹7 Lakhs/Year on Manual Processes

Rajesh Kumar (name changed) runs a mid-sized consulting firm in Mumbai with 12 employees. Like many professional services firms, he was drowning in:

- **Lead response taking 4-6 hours** - By the time his team followed up, leads had already moved on
- **Manual report generation** - 15 hours/week spent compiling client reports from multiple sources
- **Email overload** - 200+ emails daily with no system to prioritize or auto-respond
- **Client follow-ups** - Critical follow-ups falling through the cracks

His monthly operational burn was around ₹6 lakhs, with nearly 40% going to repetitive tasks that could be automated.

## The Solution: AI Automation Stack

We implemented a custom AI automation system in just 5 days:

1. **Intelligent Lead Routing** - AI qualifies and routes leads to the right consultant within 2 minutes
2. **Auto-Report Generation** - Connected their CRM, Google Sheets, and email to auto-generate weekly reports
3. **Smart Email Management** - Auto-categorization, auto-responses for common queries, and priority inbox
4. **Automated Follow-up Sequences** - 12 touchpoints handled automatically without manual intervention
