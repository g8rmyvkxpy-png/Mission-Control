Ever wonder what it'd be like to have salespeople working round the clock — never complaining, never taking sick days, costing a fraction of a human salary?

That's exactly what I built.

## The Problem: My Inbox Was Killing Me

Every morning, I'd wake up to:
- 50+ cold emails from competitors
- Leads I should've followed up with yesterday
- Competitors launching features I missed
- A to-do list that never shrank

I was working 60-hour weeks and still falling behind. Something had to change.

## The Solution: AI Agents That Actually Work

Instead of another tool or spreadsheet, I built autonomous agents that handle:

1. **Lead Finding** — Scans 50+ sources daily to find 10-25 qualified leads in my niche
2. **Outreach Writing** — Writes personalized messages for each lead (not generic templates)
3. **Competitor Monitoring** — Tracks 20+ competitors and alerts me to changes
4. **Daily Digests** — Summarizes industry news every morning so I stay informed
5. **Website Audits** — Weekly checks for SEO, speed, and broken links
6. **Report Generation** — Evening performance summaries

All of this runs while I sleep. That's not hyperbole — I check my phone at 7 AM and there's a report waiting for me.

## The Tech Stack

Here's what makes it possible:

| Component | Technology |
|-----------|------------|
| **LLM Reasoning** | MiniMax + OpenAI (GPT-4o) |
| **Memory** | Supabase + RAG for document Q&A |
| **Task Queue** | In-house agent scheduler |
| **Automation** | Puppeteer + APIs |
| **Deployment** | Node.js + PM2 |

The key insight: don't try to use one AI for everything. Use specialized agents for different tasks, with a central orchestrator coordinating them.
