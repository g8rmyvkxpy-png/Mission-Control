## What Actually Makes a Lead "Qualified"?

Before your AI can find leads, you need to define what makes someone worth pursuing. Most businesses get this wrong.

A qualified lead for a web design agency isn't "any business without a website." It's:
- A business that's currently advertising (Google Ads, Facebook)
- A business that's recently raised funding
- A business in your target geography with 10-50 employees
- A business that's been in the news

**The more specific you are, the better your AI performs.**
